Why Science is a Unique Challenge for AI

Scientific literature is a dense, multi-modal language. Unlike conversational text, it's packed with specialized jargon, structured data, mathematical equations, and complex diagrams. This section explores the composition of this unique data source and why general-purpose AI needs to adapt.
